①
EMBEDDING LOOKUP
Genres and tags are looked up in 32-dim and 64-dim tables built from Last.fm co-occurrence data.
②
PCA PROJECTION
Four PCA transforms reduce embeddings to 2D (position) and 3D (RGB). tanh stretching spreads blobs across the canvas.
③
BLOB SYNTHESIS
Each genre/tag places a radial blob with quadratic falloff. Blobs accumulate additively on a float32 canvas.
④
POST-PROCESSING
Lightness capped at 0.78, gamma 0.85 applied per channel. Output is a 400x400 PNG unique to the song.
